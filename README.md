My work focuses on building end-to-end AI systems — from multi-agent pipelines and RAG architectures to computer vision and time series forecasting.

## Projects

### Agentic AI & LLMs

**[Build-a-Deep-Research-Agent — NVIDIA Course](https://github.com/salmamoueddeb/Build-a-Deep-Research-Agent---NVIDIA-Course)**
Notebooks covering the full deployment of advanced AI research agents: NIM deployment, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), and full researcher agent pipelines. Part of NVIDIA's advanced AI curriculum.

**[Hallucination-Aware RAG System](https://github.com/salmamoueddeb/Hallucination-Aware-RAG-system)** *(Private)*
Exploration of hallucination-aware RAG applied to scientific documents. Focuses on designing, evaluating, and analyzing methods to reduce hallucination in LLM outputs over domain-specific corpora.

**[AI Agent for CV Pre-screening](https://github.com/salmamoueddeb/Agent-IA-de-pr-selection-de-CVs)**
Internship project at JOBGATE. Agentic pipeline processing 2,000+ resumes with LIME-based explainability and a recruiter-facing Streamlit dashboard. Achieved approximately 60% skill extraction accuracy.
